## Deployment Waves

The 27 agents deploy in 3 waves based on dependencies:

| Wave | Agents | Order | Total FET |
|------|--------|-------|-----------|
| **Wave 1** | C-Suite (5) | CTO → CFO → COO → CEO → CRO | 600 FET |
| **Wave 2** | Fetch Internal (7) | GUIDE, RANK, COACH, CONC, BRAND, DEV, GRANT (parallel) | 840 FET |
| **Wave 3** | SingularityNET (15) | All 15 in parallel | 1,800 FET |

**Total deployment cost:** 3,240 FET (27 × 120 FET deploy fee)

> **Why this order?** CTO first — every agent pays it for reasoning. Then financial infrastructure. Then the router. Then everything else.
